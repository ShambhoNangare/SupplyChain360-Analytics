# Business Problem

## Background

DataCo Global is a global retail company that sells products across multiple countries and markets. The company manages customer orders, inventory, shipping, and product deliveries.

Recently, the company has experienced several operational challenges that affect profitability and customer satisfaction.

## Current Challenges

- Frequent delivery delays
- Inventory shortages and overstock situations
- Increasing logistics costs
- Low profit despite strong sales
- Difficulty identifying high-performing products and regions
- Lack of a centralized dashboard for monitoring business performance

## Project Goal

As a Data Analyst, my goal is to analyze the supply chain data, identify operational bottlenecks, discover business insights, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve efficiency and profitability.
