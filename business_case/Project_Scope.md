# Project Scope

## Overview

This project focuses on performing an end-to-end analysis of a global retail company's supply chain operations using historical transactional data. The analysis aims to evaluate business performance, identify operational inefficiencies, and provide data-driven recommendations that support strategic decision-making across sales, logistics, inventory, customer management, and regional operations.

---

## In Scope

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis
- Evaluate sales trends across products, categories, markets, and regions.
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
- Measure revenue growth and sales contribution by business dimensions.

### 2. Profitability Analysis
- Analyze profit distribution across products, categories, markets, and customer segments.
- Evaluate the relationship between discounts and profitability.
- Identify products and regions generating low or negative profit.

### 3. Customer Analytics
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and buying patterns.
- Identify high-value customers and customer segments.
- Evaluate customer contribution to revenue and profitability.

### 4. Order Performance Analysis
- Analyze order volume, order status, and order trends.
- Measure average order value and purchasing patterns.
- Evaluate order distribution across different business regions.

### 5. Shipping & Delivery Performance
- Measure delivery performance using actual and scheduled shipping time.
- Analyze late delivery trends and associated operational risks.
- Compare shipping methods based on delivery efficiency.

### 6. Product Performance Analysis
- Evaluate product and category performance using sales, profit, and order quantity.
- Identify products requiring inventory optimization.
- Assess the business impact of discounts on product performance.

### 7. Geographic & Market Analysis
- Compare business performance across countries, states, cities, regions, and markets.
- Identify high-growth and underperforming business locations.
- Visualize regional business performance using geographic data.

### 8. Executive KPI Dashboard
Develop an interactive dashboard providing business stakeholders with real-time visibility into key operational metrics and business performance indicators.

### 9. Predictive Analytics
- Forecast future product demand using historical sales trends.
- Predict late delivery risk using operational variables.

---

## Out of Scope

The following areas are excluded from this project due to data limitations:

- Supplier Performance Analysis
- Procurement Analysis
- Manufacturing Operations
- Warehouse Capacity Planning
- Vehicle Routing Optimization
- Real-time Shipment Tracking
- Marketing Campaign Performance
- Financial Accounting Analysis
