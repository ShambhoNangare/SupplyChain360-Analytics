Business Objectives
Project Objective
The primary objective of this project is to analyze end-to-end supply chain operations using historical business data to identify operational inefficiencies, improve decision-making, and recommend data-driven strategies that enhance business performance.

Strategic Objectives
Evaluate the overall performance of the supply chain by analyzing sales, profitability, customer orders, inventory, and logistics operations.

Identify the root causes of delivery delays and recommend strategies to improve on-time delivery performance.

Analyze inventory availability and product demand to support efficient inventory planning and reduce stock-out and overstock situations.

Measure the impact of discounts, shipping methods, and operational factors on revenue and profitability.

Identify high-performing and underperforming products, categories, customers, markets, and regions to support strategic business decisions.

Develop interactive dashboards and key performance indicators (KPIs) that provide management with real-time visibility into supply chain performance.

Apply statistical analysis and predictive analytics to forecast demand and support proactive business planning.

Provide actionable business recommendations that improve operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and overall profitability.
