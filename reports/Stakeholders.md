# Project Stakeholders

## Executive Leadership

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Business Objectives
- Monitor overall business performance.
- Improve profitability and operational efficiency.
- Support strategic business growth.

Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Revenue Growth
- Overall Business Performance

---

## Supply Chain Manager

Business Objectives
- Improve supply chain efficiency.
- Reduce delivery delays.
- Optimize inventory availability.
- Improve operational performance.

Key KPIs
- Late Delivery Rate
- On-Time Delivery Rate
- Average Shipping Time
- Order Fulfillment Performance
- Inventory Availability

---

## Sales Manager

Business Objectives
- Increase sales revenue.
- Improve product performance.
- Identify growth opportunities.
- Optimize discount strategies.

Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Market
- Average Order Value
- Product Performance

---

## Logistics Manager

Business Objectives
- Improve delivery efficiency.
- Reduce transportation delays.
- Optimize shipping methods.

Key KPIs
- Shipping Time
- Delivery Performance
- Late Delivery Percentage
- Shipping Mode Performance

---

## Inventory Manager

Business Objectives
- Maintain optimal inventory levels.
- Reduce stock-out situations.
- Improve product availability.

Key KPIs
- Product Availability
- Inventory Status
- Order Quantity
- Product Demand

---

## Regional Business Manager

Business Objectives
- Monitor regional business performance.
- Identify high-performing and underperforming markets.
- Support regional business expansion.

Key KPIs
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region
- Customer Distribution
- Market Performance

---

## Data Analyst

Project Responsibilities
- Collect and prepare data.
- Perform exploratory data analysis.
- Develop business KPIs.
- Build predictive models.
- Create Power BI dashboards.
- Deliver actionable business recommendations.
